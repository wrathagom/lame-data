Dark-first: The brand is designed for dark backgrounds. The primary green #22c55e is calibrated for contrast against near-black surfaces. On light backgrounds, use #16a34a for the green and #dc2626 (red) instead of #f59e0b (amber) for the deviation series.

Logo Usage

Radar chart: The icon is a hexagonal radar chart with two overlapping data series — a solid green baseline and a dashed deviation line (amber on dark, red on light). The shape represents multi-dimensional gait analysis.

Lockup: The horizontal lockup places the radar chart icon to the left of a two-line wordmark. "LAME" and "DATA" are stacked, never placed on a single line.

Spacing: Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "D" in "DATA" on all sides of the logo.

Minimum size: The full lockup should not be displayed smaller than 120px wide. The standalone icon should not be smaller than 24px.
